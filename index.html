Open software and hardware for the Internet of Living Things.

We'll be launching soon! Sign up to the mailing list to keep in touch. If you're a developer feel free to try out Grow-IoT on Github.

Common Garden helps you to optimize your growing environments outdoors and indoors.

We make it easy to setup sensors and actuators and give you the feedback and instructions you need to grow anything, anywhere.

Our Grow App provides a way to organize and keep track of your growing environments and the living organisms in them. It can you help spot and resolve problems before they become problems.

Hardware developers can use our Grow-IoT framework and app for making their own custom devices.

Common Garden is being be built on standards and decentralized open source technology that will make it scalable, trusted, fair, and secure.

We hope to collaborate with others such as the MIT Open Agriculture Initiative, so the best agricultural technologies and knowledge spreads around the world as quickly as possible for the benifit of all.

Our mission: To help usher in the next agricultural revolution through open knowledge and open technology.
