Creating the next agricultural revolution

Common Garden is aiming to build open source software and hardware to make indoor farming more efficient and easier than ever before.

We hope to help usher in a future where we can grow more food with less water, less land, less toxic chemicals, less transport distance, less suffering, less energy, less CO2, less hassle, less time, less seasonality... and it can still be more nutritious, and more delicious, too.
