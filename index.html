COMMON GARDEN

Our mission is simple: To help usher in the next agricultural revolution through open knowledge and open technology.

Common Garden is making open IoT software and hardware for controlled environment agriculture. We want to create ecosystems rather than platforms, decentralized and diverse food systems as opposed to monoculture, and a true Internet of things rather than electronic silos.

We'll be launching products and an Aplha of our Grow-IoT stack soon! Sign up to the mailing list to keep in touch. If you're a developer feel free to try out Grow-IoT on Github.
